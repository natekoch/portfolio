ABOUT ME

I am a fourth-year computer science major with a minor in audio production at the University of Oregon. While I am currently residing in Eugene, Oregon, I was born and raised in beautiful Portland, Oregon. From a young age, I have always found myself drawn to figuring out how computers and various pieces of technology work. I discovered my passion for computer programming in late middle school. In addition to my passion for computers, I have found a love for music. I have become an avid listener and creator and enjoy adding to my vinyl collection. I invest time into audio production and sound design through analog and digital methods. I also love exploring nature with the Canon F-1 my late-grandfather gave to me years ago. I have showcased some of my adventures on the photography page of this website.
